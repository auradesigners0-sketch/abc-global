Our History & Growth

Assembly of Believers Church was founded in 2016 by Bishop Dickson Cornel Kabigumila in Kahama, starting with a small congregation under a tree. Driven by a vision for revival, it has since grown into a nationwide ministry with branches across Tanzania.

We are now headquartered in Kinzudi, Dar es Salaam, dedicated to spreading hope, mentorship, and gospel through aggressive outreach and digital evangelism.
