About Us

Our History, Values, and Ministries

Our History & Foundation

Assembly of Believers Church (ABC) was established in 2016 under visionary leadership of Bishop Dickson Cornel Kabigumila. Our journey began in humble town of Kahama, Tanzania, where a small congregation gathered faithfully under a tree to seek the face of God. From these modest beginnings, a powerful move of the Spirit was ignited.

Through divine grace and unwavering faith, what started as a local fellowship has blossomed into a thriving nationwide ministry. Today, we are headquartered in Kinzudi, Dar es Salaam, with branches spanning across the nation. We are committed to raising a generation that is holy unto God, fully equipped to impact the world with the Gospel.

At ABC, we believe in the demonstration of the Kingdom through signs, wonders, and miracles. We are not just a church; we are a spiritual family dedicated to walking in divine purpose, where the supernatural becomes natural, and destinies are awakened.

Raising the Next Generation
